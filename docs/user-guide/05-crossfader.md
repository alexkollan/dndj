# 5. The Crossfader

[← Prev: Decks & Playback](./04-decks-and-playback.md) · [Back to User Guide](./README.md) · [Next: Playlists →](./06-playlists.md)

---

The crossfader sits between Deck A and Deck B and controls the **balance** between
them. It's how you move seamlessly from one mood to another — fade combat down as
exploration rises, with no awkward silence or abrupt cut.

> The crossfader only affects **Deck A and Deck B**. [Deck C](./04-decks-and-playback.md#deck-c-the-third-deck)
> and the [sampler pads](./07-sampler-pads.md) are independent.

## Using it

- **Drag the slider** toward **A** (left) to favour Deck A, toward **B** (right)
  to favour Deck B. The live percentage for each side is shown above.
- **Two-finger horizontal swipe** over the crossfader nudges it (trackpad).
- **CTR** button — instantly recentre to 50/50.
- Keyboard: **`[`** nudges toward A, **`]`** nudges toward B, **`\`** centres.

A typical mood change: start a new track on the quiet deck, then slowly drag the
crossfader across. The outgoing track fades out as the incoming one fades in.

## Curve types

How the two sides fade as you move the slider is set by the **curve**. Four
options sit under the slider; hover any of them to preview its shape:

| Curve | Feel | Use it for |
|-------|------|-----------|
| **Natural** (default) | Equal-power — constant total loudness across the whole sweep | Most situations; smoothest perceived blend |
| **Slow** | Stays near the current deck longer, then moves quickly | Long, gradual transitions with minimal bleed at the extremes |
| **Linear** | Plain straight A↔B fade | Simple, predictable mixing |
| **Cut** | Both decks stay at full until the centre, then cut | DJ-style hard cuts; layering both decks at full volume around the middle |

**Natural** is the right choice almost all the time — it keeps the overall volume
steady so a transition doesn't dip or surge in the middle. Reach for **Cut** when
you actually want both decks blasting together near centre.

## A note on volume layering

What you hear from Deck A or B is the product of three controls:

```
final volume = deck VOL slider  ×  crossfader position (for that side)  ×  MASTER
```

So if a deck seems too quiet, check all three — its own volume, the crossfader,
and the master level in the top bar.
